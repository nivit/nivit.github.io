Instructions

  • Select symbols and/or expressions from the menus of the editor below or write your formula directly in the CODICE (code) textarea
  • The mark ? (question mark) is where the next symbol, number, etc. will be inserted.
  • The next mark is selected automatically by pressing the key TAB
  • If the formula that appears in the ANTEPRIMA (preview) box is whatyou want, then click the INSERISCI (Insert) button
  • If you want to restart or insert a new formula, click CANCELLA (delete)
